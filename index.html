About Us

Sky Home Improvements is a locally-owned cabinetry and woodworking shop based in Lebanon, Oregon. We specialize in custom-built cabinetry, furniture, and remodel projects with a focus on craftsmanship and detail. Whether it’s a full kitchen rebuild or a one-of-a-kind storage solution, we take pride in every cut, joint, and finish.

  • Built-In Shelves & Storage Solutions
